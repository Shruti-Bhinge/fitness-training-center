Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results with the nutrition plan?

Most clients begin noticing positive changes in energy levels, digestion, and sleep quality within the first 2 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically become apparent after 4-6 weeks of consistent adherence to the plan. The rate of progress depends on your starting point, consistency, and how closely you follow the recommendations.

Can the plan accommodate my food allergies or intolerances?

Absolutely. During your initial assessment, we thoroughly review all food allergies, intolerances, and sensitivities. Your personalized plan will exclude any problem foods while ensuring you still receive all necessary nutrients from alternative sources. We have experience working with gluten intolerance, lactose intolerance, nut allergies, and other common dietary restrictions.

What if I don't have time to cook complicated meals?

We specialize in creating simple, time-efficient meal plans. Your plan will include:

  • Quick 15-minute meal options
  • Batch cooking strategies
  • Healthy takeout alternatives
  • Minimal-prep meals
  • Ready-to-eat healthy snacks

We'll work with your schedule to create a realistic plan you can stick to.

How does this differ from free diet plans I can find online?

Our nutrition plans offer several key advantages over generic online plans:

  • Personalization: Tailored to your unique body, metabolism, and lifestyle
  • Professional oversight: Designed and monitored by certified nutrition experts
  • Adaptability: Adjusted weekly based on your progress and feedback
  • Comprehensiveness: Addresses not just what to eat but also timing, portions, and food combinations
  • Support: Direct access to a nutrition professional for questions and guidance
Do I need to take supplements with the plan?

Our primary focus is helping you meet your nutritional needs through whole foods. However, we may recommend specific supplements if:

  • You have diagnosed deficiencies (like Vitamin D or B12)
  • You have increased needs due to athletic training
  • Certain nutrients are difficult to obtain from your preferred diet (like omega-3s for vegans)

Any supplement recommendations will be evidence-based and clearly explained.
