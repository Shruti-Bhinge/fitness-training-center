Frequently Asked Questions

How is personal training different from group classes?

Personal training offers several key advantages:

  • 100% individualized attention - the workout is designed specifically for your body and goals
  • Customized programming - exercises selected based on your abilities, limitations, and preferences
  • Real-time form correction - immediate feedback to prevent injury and maximize effectiveness
  • Personalized progress tracking - adjustments made based on your unique response to training
  • Accountability and motivation - dedicated coach keeping you on track
How often should I train with a personal trainer?

The ideal frequency depends on your goals, experience level, and budget:

  • Beginners: 2-3 sessions per week to learn proper form and build foundational strength
  • Intermediate: 1-2 sessions per week with homework workouts
  • Advanced: 1 session per week for program updates and technique refinement
  • Special goals: 3-5 sessions per week for competition prep or rehabilitation

Most clients see best results with our Transform Package (8 sessions/month).

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session includes:

  1. Comprehensive assessment: Movement screening, posture analysis, and baseline measurements
  2. Goal setting: Detailed discussion about your objectives, timeline, and preferences
  3. Initial workout: Light introductory session to evaluate your current fitness level
  4. Program outline: Overview of your customized training approach
  5. Q&A: Opportunity to address any concerns and ask questions

Come dressed for exercise and be prepared to discuss your health history.

Can personal training help with injury rehabilitation?

Yes, our trainers work closely with physical therapists and doctors to create safe, effective programs for clients recovering from injuries. Benefits include:

  • Targeted exercises to restore strength and mobility
  • Movement correction to prevent re-injury
  • Gradual progression back to full activity
  • Pain management strategies
  • Improved body mechanics

Note: We require medical clearance for post-rehab training.

How quickly will I see results?

Results vary based on your starting point, consistency, and program adherence, but typical timelines are:

  • 2-4 weeks: Improved energy, better sleep, increased stamina
  • 4-8 weeks: Noticeable changes in strength and body composition
  • 3-6 months: Significant transformation visible to others
  • 6-12 months: Complete lifestyle change and habit formation

Most clients report feeling differences within the first few sessions and seeing visible changes within 6-8 weeks.
